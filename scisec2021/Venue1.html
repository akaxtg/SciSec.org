Virtual Venue

The meeting will be held on the Zoom virtual platform. You must register to gain access to the platform. Log in particulars will be sent to all registrants on Fridy, August 13 (Beijing Time).

Tips for a great experience at the virtual meeting:

  • Join from a compatible browser. Zoom works best on Chrome and Firefox.
  • Ensure a solid internet connection and network. We recommend using a laptop or PC, instead of a phone or a tablet. Ideally your internet speed should be at least 5 Mbps download and 2 Mbps upload. Test your speed here. Keep in mind that firewalls and VPNs can impact your ability to access the event.
  • Join a Zoom meeting without user account. You can join a meeting by clicking the meeting link or going to join.zoom.us and entering in the meeting ID (Which will be sent to you via your registered email address). A Zoom account is not required if you are strictly joining Zoom Meetings as a participant. Learn more about Zoom.
  • Find Help. For any question about using of the virtual platform, do not hesitate to email the organizers at SciSec2021@gmail.com.
