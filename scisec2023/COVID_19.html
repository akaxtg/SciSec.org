Dear SciSec Community,

Due to the intensification of the domestic COVID-19 situation in China, and according to the latest anti-epidemic policy of Fudan University, we are sorry to inform you that the conference will be held totally online.

The registration of online conference is free of charge. To gain access to the meeting, please register below (or English version) to receive credentials for the meeting platform. For the attendee who has register and pay for the off-line participation, will be refunded unconditionally and informed. However, it sorry that the bank transit fee and credit card fee that caused cannot be included in the refund.

The oral presentations will take place on line. The presenter should prepare their lecture video (in mp4 format) with duration less than 15 minutes and send it to SciSec2021@gmail.com by August 10. For any question about the on-line presentation, do not hesitate to contact us by SciSec2021@gmail.com.

The poster presentation will be take place online, too. The presenter should prepare one-page slide in the pdf format and send it to SciSec2021@gmail.com by August 10.

We will send you another email on the details of online presentation very soon.

Once again, we apologize for the cancellation of the offline conference, and thank you very much for your understanding and support.

We are looking forwards to seeing you online on August 13 to 15, 2021.

Best regards,
