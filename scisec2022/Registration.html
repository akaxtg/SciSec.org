Registration

SciSec 2022 will be held in Matsue city, Shimane, Japan, from Aug. 10 to Aug. 12, 2022. Anyone related to the area of cybersecurity are encouraged to register the conference as soon as possible. Note that for each accepted paper, at least one author should register the conference before July. 12, 2022.

Registration Methods

For Participants from areas and countries, please click on the Registration. SciSec 2022 entrusts Conference Service Inc. with the registration process. If you use credit card for the payment, the organization name appeared in your credit card statement is "Conference Service Inc.".

If you have any questions about registration, please contact conference affairs via chsu@u-aizu.ac.jp.
